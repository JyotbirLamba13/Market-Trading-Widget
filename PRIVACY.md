# Privacy Policy — Market Ticker

Last updated: April 2026

Market Ticker ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that
displays real-time NSE and BSE stock prices in a floating overlay.

## Data we collect
We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.

## Data stored locally
The Extension uses chrome.storage.local to store the following on
your device only:
- Your watchlist (list of stock symbols you've added)
- Your pinned symbols (up to 4)
- Widget position, state (full/mini/icon), and theme preference
- Whether the widget is currently hidden

This data never leaves your browser. It is not synced to any server.

## Network requests
The Extension makes network requests to one domain only:
- scanner.tradingview.com — to fetch live price data for the
  symbols in your watchlist.

These requests contain only the ticker symbols you've added. No
identifiers, no analytics, no tracking.

## Third parties
Price data is provided by TradingView's public scanner API. We do
not control TradingView's privacy practices. Refer to TradingView's
own privacy policy for details on how they handle requests.

## No tracking
The Extension does not use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or any other
tracking service. There are no advertising IDs, no fingerprinting,
no behavioural analytics.

## Account-free
The Extension has no login. We do not have user accounts and cannot
identify any individual user.

## Changes
If this policy changes, the updated version will be published in the
GitHub repository linked from the extension's listing.
